My Final Cut Pro keyboard commands.

Final Cut Pro > Commands > Customize... (Alt + Cmd + K):
  Blade: C
  Enable/Disable Clip: B
  Go to Next Frame: S
  Go to Previous Frame: F
  Play Rate 2: R
  Play Rate 4: Y
  Redo Changes: Shift + Z
  Snapping: 5
  Trim End: V
  Trim Start: X
  Trim Tool: T
  Undo Changes: Z
  Zoom In: E
  Zoom Out: D
  Zoom to Fit: Cmd + D
  Zoom to Samples: Cmd + E
