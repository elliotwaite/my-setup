I use this app to set up custom snap areas for window positioning, to
customize the touch bar, and to create custom trackpad gestures.

Basic Settings > [x] Launch BetterTouchTool on startup

Gestures/Triggers

  Touch Bar:

    TouchBar Button:
      Name: Esc
      Predefined Action: ESC (Escape Key, respects pressed modifiers)
      Extra padding (inside): 64
      Item Placement: Stick to the left side of the Touch Bar

    Widget/Gesture:
      Widget: Brightness Slider
      Slider width: 480 px
      Item Placement: Stick to the right side of the Touch Bar
      Knob Icon: (use a transparent image)
      [ ] Draw Knob Background

    Widget/Gesture:
      Widget: Volume Slider
      Slider width: 480 px
      Item Placement: Stick to the right side of the Touch Bar
      Knob Icon: (use a transparent image)
      [ ] Draw Knob Background

  Trackpad:

    3 Finger Swipe Up: Mission Control
    3 Finger Swipe Down: Application Expose
    3 Finger Swipe Left: Resize window to Left Two Thirds
    3 Finger Swipe Right: Resize window to Right Third
    4 Finger Swipe Up: Maximize Window
    4 Finger Swipe Left: Maximize Window Left
    4 Finger Swipe Right: Maximize Window Right

  Keyboard Shortcuts

    F1: Brightness Down
    F2: Brightness Up
